Al faham

Al Faham Chicken Recipe is a popular Arabian grilled chicken prepared with chicken marinated in Arabian spices, vegetables and barbequed in a charcoal grill or oven. Try this middle eastern delicacy Al Faham Chicken. In Arabic it is known as Dajaj Al faham and usually served with Bukhari Rice or Kabsa.

Shawarma

Shawarma is a dish in Middle Eastern cuisine consisting of meat cut into thin slices, stacked in a cone-like shape, and roasted on a slowly-turning vertical rotisserie or spit.

Mandi

Mandi is a traditional dish originated from Hadhramaut, Yemen, consisting mainly of meat and rice with a special blend of spices. It is cooked in a pit underground.
